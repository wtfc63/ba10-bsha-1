Detailed Description

Implementation of a candidate View for our IME. Could be used to show suggested word-completions for our input. Currently not used for our Input Method, have a look at the SoftKeyboard example in the SDK to see how it would be done.

Copied from the SoftKeyboard example from the Android SDK without modifications.
