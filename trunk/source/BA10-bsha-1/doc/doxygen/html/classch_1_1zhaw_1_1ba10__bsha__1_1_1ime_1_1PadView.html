Detailed Description

Implementation of a KeyboardView to gather data for the DetectionService.

Notifies its IObservers on input events such as new TouchInputs, crossing out of existing TouchInputs (seen as a backspace) or starting of new TouchInputs with a certain offset (seen as a space).
